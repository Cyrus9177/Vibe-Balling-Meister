Master the Ball

Vibe-Balling-Meister is an addictive match 3 puzzle game where precision meets rhythm. Launch balls strategically to match three or more in a line, creating explosive chain reactions and earning incredible scores.

From ancient times, people have been fascinated by games with balls. Vibe-Balling-Meister brings this classic gameplay to the digital age with stunning visuals, engaging mechanics, and multiple game modes to keep you entertained for hours.

Fast-Paced Action

Experience intense gameplay with smooth animations and responsive controls

Vibrant Visuals

Beautiful colorful balls with stunning visual effects and transitions

Challenging Levels

Progressive difficulty with unique obstacles and power-ups

Precision Gameplay

Master strategic ball placement for maximum combo potential

How to Play

1

Aim: Position your ball launcher at the bottom

2

Launch: Fire balls at the correct angle and power

3

Match: Connect 3 or more balls of the same color

4

Combo: Create chain reactions for massive points

About This Project

Vibe-Balling-Meister is a final project for the Web Systems and Technologies subjects (9317 & 9318). Our task was to create an engaging Match 3 genre game that demonstrates our proficiency in game development and web technologies.
