Master the Ball

Vibe-Balling-Meister is an addictive match 3 puzzle game where precision meets rhythm. Launch balls strategically to match three or more in a line, creating explosive chain reactions and earning incredible scores.

From ancient times, people have been fascinated by games with balls. Vibe-Balling-Meister brings this classic gameplay to the digital age with stunning visuals, engaging mechanics, and multiple game modes to keep you entertained for hours.

Orb Chain Clearing

Match three or more orbs in a row to remove them from the path.

Score Tracking

Watch your score rise as each successful match adds points.

Win and Retry States

Finish the round, then restart with SPACE or R when the game ends.

Precise Aiming

Launch orbs carefully along the path and use combos to keep the chain under control.

How to Play

1

Aim: Position your launcher and line up the shot

2

Launch: Fire orbs into the moving chain

3

Match: Connect 3 or more orbs of the same color

4

Combo: Trigger chain clears for bigger score gains

About This Project

Vibe-Balling-Meister is a final project for the Web Systems and Technologies subjects (9317 & 9318). Our task was to create an engaging Match 3 style orb puzzle game that demonstrates our proficiency in game development and web technologies.

The current build focuses on a single score-based challenge with orb matching, combo clearing, win and game-over states, and a retry flow instead of multiple levels or power-ups.
